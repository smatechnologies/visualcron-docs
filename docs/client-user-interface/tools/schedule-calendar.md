## Schedule - Calendar

The main menu **Tools > Schedule > Calendar** option presents an overview of scheduled Jobs. This way you can, for example, plan your Tasks (so they don't run at the exact same time).
 
**Tools > Schedule > Calendar**

![](../../../static/img/Client%20User%20Interface/Main%20Menu/Tools/Calendar.png)

**View**

You can switch between *Day*, *Week* and *Month* views. The *Today* button returns the view to the current date, and a mini calendar lets you navigate to a specific date. A zoom slider in the status bar adjusts the detail of the view.

**Time exceptions**

The *Show Time exceptions* and *Use Time exceptions* options control whether time exceptions are displayed and taken into account in the schedule.

**Jobs**

A list of Jobs is shown to the side, with a filter field to narrow it down. Check or uncheck a Job to show or hide it in the calendar, and use the *Select all Jobs* / *Deselect all Jobs* button to toggle them all at once. Right click a Job to *Open Job* or *Hide Job*. Hover over a scheduled item to see its name, start and end time and duration.

**Print**

The *Print preview*, *Page setup* and *Select printer* options let you print the calendar.

**Permission**

To block a user from viewing the Job report you need to disable the Jobs Read property in [User permissions > Groups](../server/main-user-permissions).
